Feature Film

Forbidden Pilgrimage (2014)

Official Selection Cagliari International Film Festival 2017, Aljazeera International Film Festival 2015, Assilah International Film Festival 2015, TPFF 2014, LPFF 2014, BPFF 2014, Bristol Palestine Film Festival 2014.

Languages Arabic, English and Italian.

Since the early centuries of Christianity, pilgrims have been treading Jesus Christ in the Holy Land, particularly to visit the sites connected with the ministry of Jesus. 4.5 million Christian pilgrims visited Israel in 2019 up from 2.9 million in 2016. The numbers are expected to continue rising steadily. Israel promotes its current control of the Holy Land as a heavenly blessing to Christians worldwide.

However, the biblical route relevant to various religious sects is turning into an abandoned one today. This is because pilgrims flocking to the holy land from all parts of the world are being misled by the Israeli Ministry of Tourism and the Israeli agencies. The native Christians are experiencing a new age of persecution. Their holiest sites are at constant threat of attack and vandalism.
