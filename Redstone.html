Feature Film

The Red Stone (2012)

Winner TPFF 2012 - Jury Special Selection

Official Selection Istanbul International Film Festival 2013, Arab Camera Film Festival – Rotterdam 2013, TPFF 2013, LPFF 2013, BPFF 2013, Bristol Palestine Film Festival 2013, Culture Unplugged Film Festival 2015.

Languages Arabic, English, French and Turkish.

Taking its title from the characteristic “Red Stone” from which many of Jerusalem’s historic buildings are built, this investigative documentary focuses on Palestinian areas of West Jerusalem that were depopulated of their original Palestinian inhabitants after the establishment of Israel in 1948. By tracking the architectural and family histories of some of these eye-catching properties, The Red Stone introduces the buildings’ current occupants and the original owners, now barred from their homes. A story about the meaning of home and architectural heritage amongst continuous conflict and forced displacement.
