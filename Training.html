Media Training: Organize and Conduct

Trainer in an academic settings:

Training undergraduate students (senior year) at Petra University, Jordan on film-making and navigating the TV industry.
Supervisor for undergraduate student projects in both the Media School and English Literature School.

Organized and facilitated workshops by international trainers for professionals in the media industry in various domains. This included travel and housing arrangements for trainers and paricipants, securing locations and supervising daily training. Trainings included:

Two-month workshop on scriptwriting conducted by American scriptwriter and the head of The Story Ark Institute in Berlin - Germany, Keith Cunningham.
Workshop organized over two countries for professional TV anchors conducted by the Lebanese renowned TV host Zahi Wehbe.
Workshops with local trainers on subjects like: MCR and studio recording, infographic and videography.

Training-related achievements:

Part of a three-person team responsible for procuring university-wide filming equipment (bid evaluation, vendor interviews, and final selection).
Preparation and inauguration of big studio (150 cubic meters) with a separate MCR unit.
Archival research to obtain historical photos, maps and documents of traditional cities.
Produced and presented a radio show on World Music.
